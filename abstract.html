Writing Abstract

If you are preparing a research paper or grant proposal, you will need to know how to write an abstract. Here is a look at what an abstract is and how to write one.

  1. American Indian Science and Engineering Society: https://www.aises.org/sites/default/files/documents/National%20Conference/Abstract%20Guidelines%20and%20Samples.pdf

  2. How to write an abstract for a conference paper: https://www.academic-conferences.org/policies/abstract-guidelines-for-papers/

  3. Scientific Programming Committee, STD Prevention Conference 2016 : https://www.cdc.gov/stdconference/2016/how-to-write-an-abstract_v3.pdf

  4. The University of Adelaide Writing Centre: https://www.adelaide.edu.au/writingcentre/sites/default/files/docs/learningguide-writinganabstract.pdf

  5. James Hartley and Guillaume Cabanac: https://www.mdpi.com/2304-6775/5/2/11/pdf

  6. Prairie View A&M Research Symposium : http://www.pvamu.edu/cojjp/wp-content/uploads/sites/31/abstract-guidelines-1.pdf

  7. The University of Melbourne Academic Skills Services: https://services.unimelb.edu.au/__data/assets/pdf_file/0007/471274/Writing_an_Abstract_Update_051112.pdf
